<objective>
Extract the OS/daemon process-control surface — `stack`, `servicectl`, `background`, the hidden
`systemd` alias group, `service`, `worker`, and the `logs` command — into `cli/commands/` modules,
and relocate the heavy lifecycle logic (PID files, status payloads, process signaling, servicectl
tick/update/external-analytics) into `infra/runtime/` services. This is the heaviest slice and the
one carrying the uncommitted `background install`/systemd WIP, so it requires extra care.

Purpose: Move the largest non-CLI process-orchestration cluster out of `app.py` (QBL-CLI-03) while
preserving hidden commands, unit/label constants, and the in-flight WIP behavior. Tests may update
monkeypatch/import target strings so they continue patching the moved symbols in their new defining
modules; do not change test assertions or expected behavior.
Output: Three command modules, two infra lifecycle modules, and an `app.py` with the daemon
command bodies and lifecycle helpers removed.
</objective>
